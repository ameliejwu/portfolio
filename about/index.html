AMELIE WU

Amelie is a multimedia creative in pursuit of a visual language that embodies remembrance, kinship, and the ephemeral. Through photography, design, and projects, she explores transient cityscapes, childhood memories, and all that is becoming.

Alongside her creative works, she is passionate about responsive art spaces and is a strong advocate of accessible arts education. Amelie graduated from UCLA in 2025 with a B.A. in Public Affairs and Geography.
